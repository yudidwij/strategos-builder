Blocking validation

  • Weight totals must equal 1.0 in EFE, IFE, and CPM.
  • Locked derived phases cannot be edited before dependencies are approved.
  • QSPM will not show or accept `AS = 0`.

  • EFE ratings remain company-specific.
  • IFE strengths use ratings 3-4; weaknesses use 1-2.
  • Strategy outputs keep dependency and source references visible.
