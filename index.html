Problem Statement

Strategy work breaks down when analysis lives in disconnected files.

Untraceable recommendations

Teams often produce strategic ideas that cannot be traced back to approved external and internal factors.

Weak validation discipline

Weight totals, AQCD quality, and rating constraints are easy to miss when the process stays manual.

Broken phase flow

Teams jump ahead into synthesis and execution before earlier phases are approved and evidence-ready.

Executive view mismatch

Balanced Scorecard, strategy map, and summary outputs often lose their connection to the analysis base.

Framework Overview

Built around the full STRATEGOS journey from diagnosis to execution.

SOT-Drive structures the strategic workflow across input, matching, decision, and execution stages while keeping each phase reviewable, approvable, and evidence-based.

Input Stage

Diagnosis + Matrices

Intake, EFE, IFE, and CPM establish the evidence base and scoring discipline.

Matching Stage

TOWS + Positioning

SWOT/TOWS, SPACE, BCG, and IE shape strategic alternatives from approved inputs.

Decision Stage

Intersection + QSPM

Intersection Rule and QSPM rank strategies without allowing invalid AS = 0 states.

Execution Stage

BSC + Strategy Map

Balanced Scorecard and executive summary remain traceable to approved strategic themes.

14-Phase Strategic Workflow

Every phase is visible, gated, and designed to prevent premature strategy jumps.

P0

Intake & Diagnosis

Company profile, industry, products, goals, competitors, and available data.

P1

EFE Matrix

External factors, AQCD quality, company-specific ratings, and weight control.

P2

IFE Matrix

Strength and weakness scoring with rating constraints and validation guards.

P3

CPM Matrix

Critical success factor comparison against industry competitors.

P4

SWOT & TOWS

Strategy formulation that only uses approved EFE and IFE key factors.

P5

SPACE Matrix

FP, CP, SP, IP, coordinates, and quadrant logic shown in order.

P6

BCG Matrix

RMSP and IGR displayed before quadrant classification.

P7

IE Matrix

Derived from approved EFE and IFE scores, not manual re-entry.

P8

Intersection Rule

Cross-tool synthesis appears before umbrella strategy decisions.

P9

QSPM Matrix

AS values support blank or 1-4 only, never zero.

P10-P13

Balanced Scorecard

Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth perspectives stay linked to themes.

P14

Strategy Map & Summary

Cause-effect map and executive summary built only from approved prior outputs.

Feature Highlights

Strategic discipline built into the interface.

  • Approval gates lock the next phase until the current one is valid and approved.
  • Validation messaging keeps AQCD, weights, ratings, and traceability visible to the user.
  • Results preserve source badges so strategic themes and BSC objectives stay evidence-based.
